           Myanmar military announced a major cabinet reshuffle hours after the country declared the state of emergency on Monday.<br /><br />Under the cabinet reshuffle, new union ministers were appointed for 11 ministries while 24 deputy ministers were removed from their posts, the military's televised statement said. <br>
